Research Debriefing Tool

This website hosts debriefing information for online social science research. On this site, participants may manage their privacy preferences and opt out of data collection.

If you are a participant in a study, you will receive a more specific link that takes you directly to information about that study.

For more information about this tool, see the demo paper from the 2018 Computer Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing conference.
