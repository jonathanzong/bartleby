If you participate, what to expect

In this study, we ask you to imagine a hypothetical scenario where you receive a tweet recommending people to follow, and your information is included in a study on whether including women on lists of follow recommendations has an effect on how many recommendations people follow.

Next, we will show you a website that allows people to manage the privacy of the information we collect for the imaginary study. We will ask for feedback on this website with a survey.

Benefits and risks

Your answers will help us improve how we manage the privacy of people who participate in our research. We will publish our findings when this study finishes.
